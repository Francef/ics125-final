Margarita

Classic Margarita Recipe

Ingredients

  • 120 mL Tequila
  • 60 mL Cointreau
  • 45 mL lime juice
  • 1 lime wedge
  • 1 tbsp coarse salt

Method

  1. Use lime to wet the rim of two glasses, then dip each glass into the coarse salt.
  2. Shake the remaining ingredients in a cocktail shaker with ice until liquid is chilled.
  3. Pour into glasses and add ice as desired.

Margarita Mystery

While many cocktails have a definitive history, the same cannot be said for the margarita. Reminiscent of the movie Spartacus, no less than seven different people are attributed to the drink's creation. According to these various tales the cocktail was supposedly invented some time between 1930 and 1948. The location of creation is popularly attributed to Mexico, but other reports attribute its inception bars located in Texas or California. The only consistent part of these stories is the massive popularity of the drink, which continues to this very day.
